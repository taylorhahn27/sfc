Sunflower History

Welcome to the Sunflower Caffe. We are located on the historic Sonoma Plaza in the heart of the California Wine Country. Our food is simple, our ingredients are real. We use salt from the San Francisco Bay, Milk from cows grazing in the Sonoma County pastures and local organic eggs in all of our dishes. The Sunflower Caffe is located in Historic Landmark #501 - the old home of Salvador Vallejo. He ultimately lost it in a poker game, which worked out great for us.

"Keeping it Simple. Keeping it Real."

Our Take on Tipping

In lieu of accepting tips, we apply a 13% Health and Happiness surcharge to your order. This helps us pay every employee a good living wage in addition to:

revenue share in the company
medical coverage
401k with company match
paid time off

We truly appreciate your support. If you'd like to leave more or disagree with this policy please ask for a manager and we can make an adjustment to your bill.
